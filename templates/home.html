🌍 About EcoTracker

EcoTracker is a web-based platform designed to help individuals understand and reduce their carbon footprint. It tracks daily activities such as electricity usage, transportation, water consumption, and waste generation.

By converting everyday actions into carbon emissions, the system provides insights, eco scores, and personalized suggestions to encourage sustainable living. Users can monitor progress, earn eco points, and unlock rewards for eco-friendly behavior.

Our mission is simple — make sustainability easy, engaging, and accessible for everyone.
